Vessel Nomination

Seller should within agreed laycan nominate performing vessel with full particulars for Buyer's confirmation. Performing Vessel should be seaworthy Single Deck Bulk Carrier less than 25 years old, classed highest Lloyds or equivalent, fully PNI covered and suitable for Grab Discharge on 1 Safe anchorage, 1 Safe berth basis. Performing vessel shall be free from all maritime lien or maritime claim.

The vessel should be Geared and have minimum 4 cranes and hooks with each crane having minimum 20 Metric Ton (MT) safe working load and a minimum outreach of 8 meters from ship's rail available for discharge operation. The vessel must have all gears in working conditions. Cost of Shore grabs / Shore cranes / harbor mobile cranes to be entirely to the Buyer's account for discharge of cargo at discharge Port. All costs incurred for discharge of cargo at anchorage entirely to the Buyer's account. Discharging cost including all lighterage charges at inner anchorage to the account of the receiver only.

The vessel shall be classed in accordance with the Institute Classification Clause of the International Association of Classification Society (IACS) in force at the time of shipment and throughout the voyage. Vessel with flag of countries under OFAC or US sanctions as declared by Indian banks will not be used by the seller.
