This ADDENDUM is dated 24th day of March 2025 between

  1. AGRIFIELD DMCC, a Free Zone company incorporated and licensed at Dubai, UAE with its registered address at Office 3201, JBC 4, Cluster N, JLT, Dubai, UAE (the “Assignor”); and

  2. ICICI BANK LIMITED (DIFC BRANCH), a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 1956 and a banking company within the meaning of the Banking Regulation Act 1949 and having its Registered Office at ICICI Bank Tower, Near Chakli Circle, Old Padra Road, Vadodara, 390 007, Gujarat, India and acting through its office at Central Park Building, Level 27, Office Tower 27-31, Dubai International Financial Centre, P.O. Box 506529, Dubai, UAE (the "Assignee").

BACKGROUND:

This Addendum is entered into pursuant to Clause 3.5 of the Agreement (as defined below).

IT IS AGREED as follows:

1. Definitions and Construction

1.1 Definitions in this Addendum

"Agreement" means this assignment of receivables agreement entered into between the Assignor and the Assignee.

"Additional Receivables" means the receivables assigned by this addendum and which are more particularly described in Appendix 1 and which constitute part of the Receivables for the purposes of the Agreement.

1.2 Construction

Capitalised terms defined in the Agreement shall have the same meaning in this Addendum unless expressly defined in this Addendum or the context otherwise requires.

2. Additional Receivables

The Assignor hereby assigns to the Assignee all of the Assignor's right, title and interest from time to time in and to the Additional Receivables including any security or remedies thereof (but none of the Assignor's obligations or liabilities thereunder) as the same may be amended, supplemented, varied, novated or replaced from time to time and, from the date of execution of this Addendum by the Assignor, the Additional Receivables shall be subject to the provisions relating to assignment of the Receivables contained in the Agreement as if they had been expressly included in the definition of Receivables.

3.Representations and Warranties

The Assignor, as at the date of this Addendum, repeats the representations and warranties set out in Clause 5.1 of the Agreement to the Assignee.

4. Other provisions

The provisions of the Agreement shall apply to this Addendum as if fully set out in this Addendum (mutatis mutandis) and the Assignor confirms that all the terms and conditions of the Agreement continue in full force and effect.

5. Governing law

This Addendum and any non-contractual obligations arising out of or in connection with it shall be governed by English laws.

6. Jurisdiction

6.1 The Assignor irrevocably agrees for the benefit of the Assignee that the UAE courts shall have jurisdiction to hear and determine any suit, action or proceeding and to settle any disputes, which may arise out of or in connection with this Addendum (respectively Proceedings and Disputes) and, for such purposes, irrevocably submits to the jurisdiction of such courts.

6.2 The Assignor irrevocably waives any objection which it might now or hereafter have to the DIFC courts being nominated as the forum to hear and determine any Proceedings and to settle any Disputes and agrees not to claim that any such court is not a convenient and appropriate forum.

6.3 The submission to the jurisdiction of the DIFC courts shall not (and shall not be construed so as to) limit the right of the Assignee to take Proceedings against the Assignor in any other court of competent jurisdiction nor shall the taking of Proceedings in any one or more jurisdictions preclude the taking of Proceedings by the Assignee in any other jurisdiction (whether concurrently or not) if and to the extent permitted by applicable law.

6.4 The Assignor irrevocably and unconditionally:

  1. 4.1 agrees not to claim any immunity from proceedings brought by the Assignor in relation to this Addendum and to ensure that no such claim is made on its behalf;
  2. 4.2 consents generally to the giving of any relief or the issue of any process in connection with those proceedings; and
  3. 4.3 waives all rights of immunity in respect of it or its assets.
